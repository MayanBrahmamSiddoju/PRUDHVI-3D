Solar Radiation Temperature

The amount of solar energy the Earth receives from the Sun affects the overall temperature. Changes in solar radiation due to variations in the Earth's orbit, solar activity, or volcanic eruptions can lead to temperature fluctuations

Albedo

Albedo refers to the ability of a surface to reflect sunlight. Light-colored surfaces, like ice and snow, have a high albedo and reflect more sunlight, contributing to cooling. Conversely, dark surfaces, such as forests and asphalt, have a low albedo and absorb more sunlight, leading to warming.

Ocean Temperatures
