Population Growth and Poverty

Rapid population growth and poverty in certain regions can drive communities to rely heavily on forest resources leading to increased pressure on forests.

Mining

Extractive industries, such as mining for minerals, oil, and gas, can result in large-scale deforestation, as they often target resource-rich forest areas.

Forest Fires

Natural and human-induced forest fires can devastate vast forested areas, leading to immediate loss of trees and long-term damage to ecosystems.
